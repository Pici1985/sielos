My Ski Holidays

This is a basic website about my skiing holidays and the people I have shared them with. Including details about the resorts and some pictures with maps of each resort we have visited. Hope you gonna enjoy it..:)

My name is Balazs Ferencz, I`m a keen skier and have spent several seasons in variuos mountain resorts enjoying the clean air, breathtaking views, great food and drinks, and the most important of all, GREAT COMPANY!!!

Friends I have travelled with:

I have known these guys for quite a while so I can tell you we really always had a great time...

Resorts we have visited together are the following:

Grächen

Grächen is a small village in Wallais Canton is Switzerland.

  • Location: Switzerland
  • Slopes:245km
  • Lifts:14
  • Max alt:2873m

Superdevoluy

Superdevoluy is located near Lyon in the French Alps.

  • Location: France
  • Slopes:314km
  • Lifts:21
  • Max alt:3210m

Flaine

Flaine is purpose built resort in heart of the Alps.

  • Location: France
  • Slopes:345km
  • Lifts:28
  • Max alt:3173

StAnton am Arlberg

StAnton is one of the most prestigeus ski resorts in Tyrol.

  • Location: Austria
  • Slopes:415km
  • Lifts:32
  • Max alt:3342m

Tignes

Tignes is one of the highest altitude resorts of Europe.

  • Location: France
  • Slopes:387km
  • Lifts:27
  • Max height:3456m

Isola2000

Isola2000 is a purpose built, compact ski village on 2000m altitude.

  • Location: France
  • Slopes:215km
  • Lifts:17
  • Max height:2473
